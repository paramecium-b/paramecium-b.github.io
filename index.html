DREAM [F] LAB is an ongoing web directory that explores the borders between Dream and Reality. It documents my most memorable dreams using visual and auditory stimuli, THOUGH IT HAS SLOWLY EVOLVED INTO A DIGITAL DIARY THAT I KEEP GROWING UP OVER THE YEARS. Make your way through the dreams using the index ABOVE, or use the "next" button at the end of each dream entries to move back/forward.
